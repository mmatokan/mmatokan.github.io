FERate

FERate is a Quality of Experience assessment system designed to be accessible from within Virtual Reality.

This was a group project for Software Design course, and it comprised of three parts. The first part was creating a game for VR, the second part was creating a QoE system within VR, and the third part was creating a desktop system for managing and creating questionnaires and collecting and representing collected data. I was the team leader, and I worked on creating the QoE System.
